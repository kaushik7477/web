My journey

Hi, I’m Kaushik, a tech enthusiast and BCA student at Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology (2022–2025). I started my journey in tech after completing my schooling at Santipur M.N. High School (Class XII, 2021) and Santipur Municipal High School (Class X, 2019).

Over the years, I’ve picked up programming skills in C, Java, and Python, and explored web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, PHP, Django, and the MERN stack.

I love building projects that bring ideas to life—some of my favorites include an online book library, a Nike clone website, a Digha hotel booking platform, a construction company site, a car rental service, and even a Bluetooth-controlled RC car using Arduino.


Outside of coding, I’m passionate about photography, cinematography, and graphic design, which help me stay creative and think differently. Traveling and gaming are also big parts of my life, keeping me curious and inspired.

Right now, I’m looking to kickstart my career with an entry-level IT role where I can sharpen my skills, gain real-world experience, and contribute to exciting projects.
